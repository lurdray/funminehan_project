WHO WE ARE

We are The FN Foundation , a Non-profit organisation duly incorporated and registered in Nigeria under the Corporate Affairs Commissions.

Funmi Nehan Foundation was created in July 2017. At Funmi Nehan Foundation we aim to make everyone created by God smile through giving and impacting their lives.

We bridge the gap by providing for immediate needs of the less privilege ones in our society.

We ensure their daily / pressing needs are let to ensure a better living.

WHAT WE DO

Alot of families can't afford their daily meal.

Individuals are unable to start to start a trade due to lack of funds, children are unable to attend school due to lack of parental care or unable to pay their school fees.

Alot of other Non-profit organisations are unable to meet up with daily needs due to lack of funds.

We help families in such categories with provision of food, clothing, school fees and trade set up to ensure their basic daily needs are met.

We at Funmi Nehan Foundation become their support and put a smile to their face. We follow up on individuals and ensure they are well catered for.

How We Got Here

Apart from having a passion to be a blessing.

The vision was given to our founder over 3 years ago.

She step out in faith 3 years later and the rest is history Touching lives is our vision and we are totally fulfilled seeing every individual we come across smile on our little way.

Our mission is to be a Blessing to the less privileged. Life is a gift and the only way to give back to the world is to Touch Lives.

We have been to different states in Nigeria and still serving through our monthly outreach and individual giving.

We aim to continue serving/ meeting needs and touch life globally.

We are convinced that the world will eventually be a better place and the lack among us as human beings will be met if we continue serving and reaching out in love.
